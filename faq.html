General Questions
Send-In Service Questions
What is RGH modding?
RGH (Reset Glitch Hack) allows Xbox 360 consoles to run custom firmware and unsigned code.
How can I order a pre-built console or send-in service?
Simply contact us with details regarding your order to get started. If you know what you're interested in, such as a pre-built console, please provide a product link or picture of it to make the process drastically quicker.
What's the price of shipping?
We charge a flat rate of $15 for shipping. Typically, consoles are shipped with either USPS or UPS.
How long does shipping usually take?
If you're going with our most popular shipping option, USPS Priority Mail, it's estimated between 1-3 days.
Will I receive a tracking number after purchasing?
Absolutely, as soon as orders leave our hands we'll share the tracking link with the buyer.
Can you ship pre-built consoles worldwide?
At this moment, no unfortunately. But we are shipping consoles within the United States.
Can you help me setup my RGH?
Yes, all community members are welcome to use our #tech-support on Discord for any tech related questions. Although if you're a beginner, we strongly advise following RGH/JTAG guides & tutorials which can be easily found through a Google or YouTube search. Use trusted sources only!
Can I request a custom built console?
Absolutely. Bare in mind that any custom orders require a pre-payment and usually 2-5 business days to start, finish, package and ship the order. In rare cases, this wait time can be extended if supplies need to be ordered.
Do you offer warranties? If so, what does it cover?
Yes! We include a 30 day warranty with all bundle deals unless stated otherwise. Warranties begin on the console's delivery date and cover any hardware related problems.
How do I send in my console for modding?
Carefully package your Xbox 360 and ship it to our address. Include a note with your order details and contact information so we can process it quickly.
Do I need to provide accessories like controllers or power cords?
No, only the console is required. However, if you want any additional accessories installed or upgraded, include them and specify in your order note.
How long does a typical send-in service take?
Most consoles are processed and returned within 3-7 business days, depending on the service requested and our current order volume.
Can I track my console while it‚Äôs being serviced?
Yes! Once your console is received and processed, we‚Äôll send you a tracking number so you can monitor its return shipping status.
Do you offer warranties on send-in services?
Absolutely. All RGH installs done through our send-in service include a 30-day warranty covering any hardware or installation issues.
What should I do if my console arrives damaged during shipping?
Contact us immediately. We recommend using a shipping service with insurance to cover any potential damage. We‚Äôll guide you through filing a claim if necessary.
